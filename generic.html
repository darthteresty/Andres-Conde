Who am I?

I design videogames,
I write stuff
and I do voice acting

Degree in Videogame Design and Development at ENSE University Center, Madrid (Spain). For the last 7+ years I dedicated my work life to develop videogames, as QA, Dev Test and Game Designer, helping in every single process from scratch to release.

I participated in multiple games for different platforms and targets, acquiring lot of experience in Game Systems and Game Narrative, some of them as a Lead Game Designer. I also work in different personal projects as a writer and voice actor.

I currently live in Madrid (Spain), but from 2021 to 2025 I also lived in Bucharest (Romania) where I worked in Ubisoft Romania developing the post-launch content of "Tom Clancy's: The Division 2".

Between 2018 and 2021, I worked at Stage Clear Studios in Madrid (Spain), developing games for multiple platforms like "Delicious - Emily's Road Trip" (Android, iOS), "Ryan's Rescue Squad" (PS, Nintendo Switch, Xbox, PC) or "The Last Kids on Earth and the Staff of Doom" (PS, Nintendo Switch, Xbox, PC)

As I mentioned, I also spent 4 years learning in a Voice Acting School in Spain and participated in a couple of series and films. Apart from that, I also write short stories and different narrative content on my own.

I consider myself as a passionate person in my job, and I like working with other people as a team, where you can improve yourself thanks to the members of your team. I can easily express my ideas and make people understand them.
