My Work at Stage Clear Studios

2018 - Aug 2021

I started my adventure in the videogame industry at "Stage Clear Studios" back in 2018 after finishing my degree at ESNE in Videogames Design and Development.

I started my work in the company as QA and Dev Test, helping in the finding and report of bugs and ensuring a good quality in our porting services for over 30 different games and consoles (PC, Xbox One, Xbox Series X, PS4, PS Vita, Nintendo Switch and Smartphones).

Later, I was given the opportunity to take a role as Game Designer, developing a couple of games from scratch to release and post launch content, talking with different clients to develope games based on stablished frnachises, making presentations for game ideas...

In the end I was given an opportunity to lead a new game, "Ryan's Rescue Squad", which I was able to finish just before "Ubisoft Romania" contacted me and asked me to join "The Division 2" team in Bucharest.
