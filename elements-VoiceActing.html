My Voice Acting Work

2018 - Current day

As Voice Actor, I participated in multiple films and series since I started taking classes at AM Escuela, in Madrid.

I began my voice acting classes after I finished university, and participated in multiple films an series doing additional voices, but I had to stop when I started working at "Ubisoft Bucharest", although I tried to continue my exercices and routines while I was there.

Now that I'm back in Madrid, I started taking classes again and involve myself even more in this fascinating job.

As a minor disclaimer, for the moment I only worked as voice actor in my native language (spanish), but would love to try in english too if given the opportunity.

Some of the things I worked on

  • Una Confesión (2019) - Coleman
  • ¡UPSSS! 2 ¿Y Ahora Dónde Está Noé? (2020) - Voces adicionales
  • Igor (2020, TV Series) - Voces adicionales
  • Ponyo En El Acantilado (Netflix, 2020) - Voces adicionales
  • El Jinete del Dragón (2020) - Voces adicionales
